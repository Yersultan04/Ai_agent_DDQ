Encryption at Rest Policy
- All customer data stored in PostgreSQL is encrypted at rest using AES-256.
- Keys are managed via a dedicated KMS with rotation at least every 12 months.
- Backups are encrypted at rest with AES-256 and access is restricted by least privilege.
Single Sign-On (SSO)
- We support SAML 2.0 for enterprise SSO.
- Optional OIDC is available for selected integrations.
Incident Response
- 24/7 on-call. Customer notification within 72 hours for confirmed incidents impacting data confidentiality.
